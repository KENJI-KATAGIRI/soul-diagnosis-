About Kenji Katagiri

Kenji Katagiri is the founder of Gaia Arts and a human development professional with more than 30 years of experience in Japan’s beauty and service industries. His work combines practical field knowledge, people development, service quality, and a deep interest in internal alignment and decision-making.

Background

Over the course of his career, Kenji has been involved in salon management, team development, service education, and professional training. His experience has shown him that long-term results are created not only by skill, but by the relationship between mindset, communication, internal condition, and action.

Training philosophy

Kenji’s approach is grounded in practical human development. He values training that helps people not only learn concepts, but also change how they show up in work and life. His programs often connect service standards, leadership, and inner alignment in a way that supports clearer decisions and stronger performance.

For a related perspective on individual body–mind coaching in Japan, see Life Energy Coaching (Nagoya)—kept separate from Gaia Arts’ organizational and international focus.

International vision

Through Gaia Arts, Kenji is building cross-border collaborations in Asia, especially in areas related to hospitality, leadership, education, beauty, and human development. His goal is to contribute through meaningful partnerships that combine Japanese standards with local relevance.
